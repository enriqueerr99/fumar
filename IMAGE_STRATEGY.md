## Key Principles for Rewriting

1. **Replace clinical terms with feelings**: Instead of explaining WHAT happens, show HOW it FEELS
2. **Use second person**: "You will see..." not "The program shows..."
3. **Make it personal**: "Your victory", "Your family", "Your peace"
4. **Show transformation**: Paint the before/after emotional journey
5. **Use power words**: Winning, proving, believing, trusting, growing, healing
6. **Remove "protocol"**: Replace with immediate benefit language
7. **Accessibility**: Use simple, direct language anyone can understand
